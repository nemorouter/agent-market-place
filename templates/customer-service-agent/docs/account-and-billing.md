# Account & billing

Sample billing content for the Acme support agent.

## Plans

- **Free** — up to 3 projects, 5 members.
- **Pro ($12/user/mo)** — unlimited projects, advanced permissions, priority support.
- **Enterprise** — SSO, audit logs, custom contracts.

## Updating your payment method

Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment method**. We accept all major cards. Changes
take effect on your next invoice.

## Resetting your password

Click **Forgot password** on the sign-in page. You'll get a reset link by email
(valid for 1 hour). If it doesn't arrive, check spam or contact support@acme.com.

## Canceling

**Settings → Billing → Cancel plan.** You keep access until the end of the current
billing period; we don't pro-rate partial months.

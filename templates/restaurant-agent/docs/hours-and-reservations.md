# Hours & reservations — Tomato & Basil

## Hours
- **Mon–Thu:** 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- **Fri–Sat:** 12:00 PM – 11:00 PM
- **Sun:** 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM

## Location
123 Olive Lane, Downtown. Street parking after 6 PM; a public garage is one block east.

## Reservations
- Book online at https://tomatoandbasil.example/reserve.
- We hold tables for 15 minutes past the reservation time.
- Parties of 8+ — please call so we can arrange seating.
- Walk-ins welcome at the bar.

## Good to know
- Outdoor patio (seasonal, weather permitting).
- High chairs and a kids' menu available.
- Private dining room seats up to 20 — email events@tomatoandbasil.example.
